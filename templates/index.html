How It Works

Smart hiring in 4 steps.

From profile to placement, the entire process is powered by AI and designed for clarity.

1

Build your skill profile

Job seekers map skills. Employers describe roles. Both in plain language, not rigid forms.

2

AI matches on skill codes

Our engine maps skills to standardized codes and scores fit — not keyword overlap.

3

See scores and gaps

Both sides see transparent scores. Seekers see what to improve. Employers see why candidates fit.

4

Connect directly

Message, interview, and hire through the platform. No middlemen. No recruiter cuts.
