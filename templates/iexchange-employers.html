The Real Problem

Traditional hiring is
broken in 3 ways.

📥

Resume flood

A single job post gets 200+ applications. Your team manually filters 80% of them just to find 10 worth reading. That's hours of work before a single conversation happens.

🎯

Keyword matching is a lie

ATS filters for keywords, not capability. You miss the candidates who can actually do the job because they didn't write it the same way your JD did.

💸

Bad hires are expensive

A wrong hire costs 1.5x to 2x annual salary when you factor in rehiring, lost productivity, and team disruption. Most happen because the fit was never properly assessed.
