About Us

We're fixing how
hiring actually works.

i.Exchange.ai is built by Spire.AI — a team that has spent years building workforce intelligence tools for enterprises. We took that same AI and built it for individuals and growing companies who deserve the same quality of matching.

Why we exist

The job market is broken in a specific, fixable way. Employers can't find the right people even when they're there. Job seekers can't get noticed even when they're qualified. The problem isn't supply or demand — it's that the matching layer between them is built on keyword filters from the 1990s.

We built i.Exchange.ai to replace that matching layer with something that actually works — AI that understands skills, not just words.

Our matching engine maps every skill to a standardized code, then scores fit based on what people can actually do — not how they described it on a resume.

What we believe

🎯

Skills over credentials

What you can do matters more than where you studied. Our platform is built around demonstrated capability, not paper qualifications.

🔍

Transparency by default

Every match score comes with an explanation. No black boxes. You always know why you matched and what to improve.

🌍

Built for emerging markets

Most hiring platforms are built for the US and Europe. We started in India and are expanding across Asia and Africa first.

⚡

Speed matters

Every day a role goes unfilled costs money. Every day a job seeker doesn't hear back costs confidence. We optimize for both.
