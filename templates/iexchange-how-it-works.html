The AI Engine

Why skill codes beat keyword matching.

Traditional ATS systems look for the same words in your resume as in the job description. i.Exchange maps both to standardized skill codes — so a "data analyst" with "SQL" matches a role asking for "database querying", even if the words are different.

  • 🔍

    Semantic understanding

    Different words, same skill — the engine understands both sides.

  • 📊

    Depth scoring

    5 years of Python ranks higher than 6 months. Experience depth is factored in.

  • 🔄

    Continuous learning

    As you add skills or complete courses, your match scores update in real time.
