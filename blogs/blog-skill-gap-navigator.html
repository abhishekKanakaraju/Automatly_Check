Introducing the Skill Gap Navigator.

For any role you want but aren't ready for, we now show you exactly what to learn and in what order. Not a generic list. A real path from where you are to where you want to be.

One of the most common pieces of feedback we heard from job seekers in our first year was this: "I know I'm not ready for the role I want. But I don't know what to do about it."

The match score told them where they stood. What it didn't tell them was what to do next. Today that changes with the launch of the Skill Gap Navigator.

The problem with generic upskilling advice

Most platforms that offer "learning recommendations" give you a list of courses based on your job title. Data analyst? Here are 40 data science courses. That's not a path. That's a catalogue.

The Skill Gap Navigator works differently. It starts with a specific role you want, looks at the exact skill requirements for that role, compares them to your current skill profile, and builds a prioritized learning path for the gap — not for your job title in general.

The average job seeker has a 67% match on roles they apply to. With the Skill Gap Navigator, users who completed their recommended path moved to an average of 84% match on the same role in under 6 weeks.

How it works

When you find a role you want but your match score isn't where you need it, click "Show my gap" on any job listing. The navigator does three things:

  • Identifies the specific skills the role requires that you don't yet have at the required depth
  • Ranks them by how much each one will improve your match score if you close it
  • Recommends the fastest path to close each gap — courses, projects, or certifications

A real example

Say you're a frontend developer targeting a Senior Full Stack Engineer role at a fintech company. Your match score is 65%. The navigator shows you this:

Your skill gap — Senior Full Stack Engineer
React.js 92% — Strong
Node.js / Express 58% — Gap
PostgreSQL 44% — Gap
System Design 35% — Gap
CI/CD Pipelines 71% — Partial

The navigator tells you that closing Node.js and PostgreSQL gaps will move your score from 65% to 81% — enough to be competitive. System Design is important but less urgent for this specific role. You get a focused 4-week plan, not a 6-month curriculum.

How to access it

The Skill Gap Navigator is available to all job seekers on i.Exchange.ai today — free. Open any job listing, check your match score, and if there's a gap, click "Show my path". Your personalized upskilling plan loads instantly.

We'll be adding employer-facing gap reports in the next quarter — so hiring teams can see which skills are hardest to find in their target market and adjust their role requirements accordingly.

The best time to close a skill gap is before you apply. The Skill Gap Navigator makes that possible.
