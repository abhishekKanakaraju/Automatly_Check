Here's what this means, which markets we're in, and what we're building next.

Why these markets

All three new markets share a common challenge: large, skilled workforces that are underserved by existing job platforms. Keyword-based matching fails even harder in multilingual markets where candidates describe the same skills in different languages and formats.

Our skill code matching engine is language-agnostic at its core — it matches on standardized codes, not words. That makes it particularly well suited to markets where English is a second language but technical skills are world-class.

In our first 30 days in Southeast Asia, the average match score for shortlisted candidates was 79% — higher than our global average of 76% at the same stage of market launch.

What's next

We're targeting 15 countries by end of 2026. The next wave includes Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka — all markets with large, skilled talent pools and growing demand for AI-matched hiring.

If you're an employer in any of these markets and want early access, reach out to us directly through the platform.

To every job seeker and employer who joined us in the new markets — welcome. We're building this for you.
