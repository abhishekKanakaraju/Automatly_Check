Product Update

Direct messaging is now live for all users.

Shortlisted candidates can now message hiring managers directly inside i.Exchange. No recruiter middlemen, no cold email chains. Just a direct conversation.

i.Exchange Team

April 25, 2026
2 min read
💬

Starting today, every shortlisted candidate on i.Exchange.ai can message the hiring manager directly — inside the platform, with no intermediaries.

What changed

Previously, when an employer shortlisted you, the next step was typically an email from a recruiter or a generic calendar invite. Now, shortlisted candidates get a direct message thread with the hiring team inside i.Exchange.

In our beta, candidates who used direct messaging got a response 3x faster than those waiting for recruiter follow-up.

How it works

  • Employer shortlists a candidate — candidate gets notified instantly
  • A direct message thread opens between candidate and hiring manager
  • Both sides can share context, schedule interviews, and ask questions
  • Full message history is saved in the platform for reference

Why we built this

Recruiting middlemen exist because hiring at scale is hard. But for most roles, the best conversation is a direct one. When a hiring manager can ask a candidate a quick question and get an answer in minutes — not days — the whole process moves faster and feels more human.

Direct messaging is live for all users today. No setup needed — if you're shortlisted, your message thread opens automatically.
